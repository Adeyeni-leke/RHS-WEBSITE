Principal Message

Redeemer’s High School was established in 1998, as a result of the vision given to Pastor and Pastor (Mrs) E.A Adeboye. The school continues to grow spiritually and academically. This is in line with the vision of the school to raise godly children that would be Change Agents in our Society.

The school is located within the serene and the academically conducive environment of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, Redemption Camp, along the Lagos-Ibadan expressway. Aesthetically, the friendly environment helps in molding the character of learners as well as ensuring they acquire appropriate levels of literacy, numeracy, manipulative, communicative and life coping skills.

They also acquire ethical, moral and civic values required for laying a solid foundation for lifelong learning. To achieve the above, the school has a strong spiritual and moral foundation for all our students as they are further nurtured by God fearing staff.

In addition, the school has excellent facilities to complement the crop of dedicated and qualified teaching staff that has produced excellent results over the years. It is my conviction that your ward’s full potentials will be realized as you make up you mind to enrol them in Redeemer’s High School today.
