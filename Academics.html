School Curriculum



Junior Secondary School Curriculum

The Junior School runs a three-year programme which exposes students to the basic principles of sciences, Arts and Commercial subjects as preparatory for secondary education.
The following are subject offered in the junior school:

English-Language

Mathematics

French Language

Yoruba Language/Igbo Language

Social Studies

Christian Religious Studies

Computer Science

Agricultural Science

At the end of their programme students are expected to sit for two external examination: Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) and Ogun State Education Examination For their Junior School Certificates.

Secondary Secondary School Curriculum

A good number of students who complete their studies in the Junior Section continue with their academics in the Senior Section. Therefore, the students are grouped into Sciences, Arts & Humanities and Commercial departments with the professional and supportive guidance of the School counsellor.

On completion of mandatory 3-years academic period students sit for the following examination: WASSCE, NECO, Cambridge and IGSCE.

The following subjects are offered in the Senior Section:

Computer Studies

Agricultural Science

P.H.E

Business Studies

CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES



In our quest of moulding an all round student, students are encouraged to actively engage in the games and sporting activities of their own interest. Towards this end, several sporting facilities and playing field have been provided to meet and nurtured variety of interest and talent.

For the purpose of competition, the school is divided into four houses namely Zion (Purple House), Bethel (Blue House) Canaan (Yellow House), Goshen (Green House).

There are also subject based and non-subject based clubs. They include:

  • Subject Based Clubs
  • French Club
  • Mathematics/Jets Club
  • Business Studies Club
  • Young Farmers Club
  • Home Maker Club
  • Music/Choreography club
  • Creative Hands
  • Geography
  • Non Subject based clubs
  • ICT Club
  • Literacy & Debating Society/ Drama
  • Readers Club
  • Press Club
  • Red Cross, Health & Road Safety
  • Yoruba Club
  • Chess & Scrabble
  • Bible Club
  • Photographer Club
  • Taekwondo Club
  • Space Club
